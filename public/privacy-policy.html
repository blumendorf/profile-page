Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Dr Marco Blumendorf ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit blumendorf.info (the "Website"). We are committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected and we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

When you contact us through the Website, we may collect:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Any other information you choose to provide

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

We use Google Analytics to collect:

  • Usage data (pages visited, time spent)
  • Device information (browser type, operating system)
  • IP address (anonymized)

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Consent: For non-essential cookies and analytics
  • Legitimate Interest: For improving our website and services
  • Contract: When responding to your inquiries or providing requested services

4. Cookies and Tracking

4.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off. They are usually only set in response to actions made by you, such as setting your privacy preferences or filling in forms.

4.2 Analytics Cookies

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. These cookies collect information about:

  • Pages you visit
  • Time spent on each page
  • Technical information about your device and internet connection

You can control analytics cookies through our cookie consent banner.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for different periods depending on the purpose:

  • Contact form submissions: 1 year after last interaction
  • Analytics data: 26 months
  • Cookie preferences: 6 months

6. International Data Transfers

We use Google Analytics, which may transfer your data to servers outside the EU. These transfers are protected by:

  • EU Standard Contractual Clauses
  • IP anonymization
  • Data minimization practices

7. Data Protection Rights

Under GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Restriction: Limit how we use your data
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw previously given consent

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below. We will respond to all requests within 30 days.

8. Data Protection Officer

While we are not required to have a Data Protection Officer, for any privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights, please contact:

Dr Marco Blumendorf
Email: marco@blumendorf.info

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page.
